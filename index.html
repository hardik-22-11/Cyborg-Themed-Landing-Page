// The Procedure

Integration happens in order. Skipping one breaks the seam.

Five phases, roughly six weeks, fully reversible up until phase four.

01
Week 1

Baseline Scan

Full-body mapping of nerve density, bone structure, and existing scar tissue. This becomes the template every module is fitted against.

02
Week 2

Module Selection

You choose function over form. We simulate each combination against your baseline before anything is fabricated.

03
Weeks 3–4

Fabrication & Fitting

Components are grown and machined in parallel, then fitted in staged sessions so your body never has to relearn itself all at once.

04
Week 5

Seam Calibration

The point of no return. Organic and synthetic signals are tuned to agree on a shared response time.

05
Week 6

Discharge & Monitoring

You leave with full function. We keep reading your vitals remotely for 90 days to catch drift early.
